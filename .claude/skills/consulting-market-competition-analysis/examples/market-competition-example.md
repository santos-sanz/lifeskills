# Example: mid-market HR software

## Scope and market definition
- Market boundary: HRIS for 200-2,000 employee firms
- Geography: North America
- Segments: tech, healthcare, retail

## Market sizing
- TAM: 6.5B annual software spend
- SAM: 3.1B for cloud HRIS
- SOM: 250M near-term target

## Competitive landscape
- Key competitors: Workday, BambooHR, UKG
- Substitutes: payroll suites, PEOs

## Positioning and differentiation
- Positioning matrix: price vs depth of HR features
- Differentiation: faster implementation, mid-market UX
