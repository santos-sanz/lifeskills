# Example: portfolio review

## Portfolio scope
- Business units: Core SaaS, Services, Legacy on-prem
- Metrics: revenue growth, margin, share
- Time horizon: 3 years

## Framework and scoring
- Framework chosen: GE / McKinsey 9-cell
- Scoring criteria: market growth, profitability, share, capability fit

## Resource allocation
- Invest: Core SaaS
- Hold: Services
- Harvest: Legacy on-prem

## Growth moves
- Core SaaS: enter adjacent vertical, partner channel
- Services: bundle with SaaS for retention
