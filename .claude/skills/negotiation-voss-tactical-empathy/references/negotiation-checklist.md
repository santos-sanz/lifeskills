# Negotiation checklist

## Before the conversation
- Objective, minimum acceptable, and walk-away defined
- Counterparty map and decision makers identified
- Likely objections and accusation audit drafted
- Calibrated questions prepared
- Concession list with trade rules
- Evidence and benchmarks ready

## During the conversation
- Use labels to surface emotions
- Mirror key words to encourage detail
- Ask how/what questions, avoid why
- Listen for new constraints or black swans
- Summarize to reach "that's right"

## Close and follow-up
- Confirm next steps and owners
- Restate agreements in writing
- Set checkpoints and timeline
