# Example: upgrade email

## Objective and audience
- Objective: move free users to paid plan
- Audience: small team leads
- Desired action: start 14-day trial

## Principles selected
- Social proof: 1,200 teams upgraded this quarter
- Authority: quote from analyst report
- Scarcity: trial includes limited onboarding slots

## Message draft
- Hook: "Join the 1,200 teams who upgraded in Q1"
- Value: "Cut reporting time by 30 percent"
- Proof: "Top analyst report ranks us #1 in workflow reliability"
- Call to action: "Start your 14-day trial"
