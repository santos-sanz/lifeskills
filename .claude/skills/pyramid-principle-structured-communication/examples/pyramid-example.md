# Example: pricing recommendation

## Governing question
- Question: Should we increase prices this quarter?
- Audience: Executive team

## Answer first
- Key message: Increase prices by 5 percent on enterprise tier to offset cost inflation without hurting volume.

## Supporting points (MECE)
1. Demand remains strong in enterprise segment.
2. Competitors have already moved pricing.
3. Cost inflation has reduced margin below target.

## Evidence per point
- Demand: pipeline and renewal rates stable.
- Competition: two peers announced increases.
- Costs: hosting and support costs up 12 percent.
