# Example: profitability decline

## Problem statement
- Statement: Why did profit decline in Q2?
- Metric and baseline: Profit down 15 percent vs Q1
- Time horizon: Last two quarters

## Tree type
- Driver

## Issue tree (ASCII)
Profit
|- Revenue
|  |- Price
|  |- Volume
|     |- New customers
|     |- Retention
|- Cost
   |- Variable cost per unit
   |- Fixed cost

## Priority branches
1. Volume: retention decline
2. Variable cost per unit
3. Price discounting

## Analyses and data needs
- Retention cohorts, churn reasons, segment mix
- Cost breakdown by supplier and SKU
- Discount rate by channel and segment
