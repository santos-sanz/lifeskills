# Competitor profile checklist

## Basics
- Company size and revenue range
- Core products and target segments
- Geography and channels

## Offering and positioning
- Pricing and packaging
- Differentiation claims
- Feature gaps vs peers

## Capabilities
- Technology and IP
- Distribution and partnerships
- Operational strengths

## Economics
- Unit economics and margin signals
- Cost structure hints

## Traction signals
- Growth rate, funding, hiring
- Customer references and logos
