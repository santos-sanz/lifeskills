# Competitor profile checklist with signal quality

## 1) Basics
- Company scale and target segment.
- Core offering and deployment model.
- Geographic footprint and channel model.

## 2) Value proposition
- Primary promise to buyers.
- Differentiation claims and proof artifacts.
- Known weaknesses in target segment.

## 3) Commercial model
- Pricing and packaging structure.
- Discount behavior and contract patterns.
- Sales motion and cycle length clues.

## 4) Capability signals
- Product depth in core workflows.
- Integration ecosystem and partner strength.
- Brand trust indicators in target vertical.

## 5) Economics and traction
- Growth and retention signals.
- Margin clues and cost intensity indicators.
- Hiring and GTM expansion momentum.

## 6) Threat rating
Rate each competitor on:
- Overlap intensity (low/medium/high)
- Win probability risk (low/medium/high)
- Response speed if we enter (low/medium/high)

## Evidence quality label
For each major claim, tag source quality as high, medium, or low.
