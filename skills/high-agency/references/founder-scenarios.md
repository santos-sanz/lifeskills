# Founder scenarios playbook

## Hiring under constraints
Default moves:
- Define role outcomes for 90 days instead of pedigree filters.
- Narrow search to high-signal channels and operator referrals.
- Run paid work tests before long interview loops.

Anti-patterns:
- Waiting for a perfect candidate profile.
- Delegating hiring criteria without ownership.

First 24h actions:
- Publish a scorecard with 3 outcome metrics.
- Send 10 targeted outreach messages with specific role outcomes.
- Schedule 2 practical work-test interviews.

## Product launch blocked by dependencies
Default moves:
- Re-scope launch to a dependency-light core slice.
- Create parallel tracks: unblock critical path while shipping partial value.
- Escalate blockers with binary decision asks.

Anti-patterns:
- All-or-nothing launch scope.
- Endless coordination meetings without decisions.

First 24h actions:
- Map dependency graph and mark critical path owners.
- Define a fallback launch scope that can ship in 72h.
- Send decision memo to stakeholders with one requested decision per blocker.

## Revenue or runway pressure
Default moves:
- Prioritize cash-generating activities over roadmap comfort.
- Segment accounts/opportunities by probability and time-to-cash.
- Cut low-leverage spend and reallocate to near-term revenue actions.

Anti-patterns:
- Spreading effort across too many initiatives.
- Protecting sunk-cost projects with weak payback.

First 24h actions:
- Build a 6-week cash view with best/base/worst scenarios.
- Rank top 20 revenue opportunities by closability.
- Launch a daily revenue standup with owners and next moves.

## Team misalignment or conflict
Default moves:
- Clarify decision rights and single-threaded owners.
- Force explicit tradeoffs in writing.
- Resolve conflicts at the objective/constraint level, not personality level.

Anti-patterns:
- Consensus theater and unclear accountability.
- Re-litigating decisions without new evidence.

First 24h actions:
- Publish one-page decision-rights map.
- Run a conflict reset with objective, constraints, options, decision, owner.
- Set a weekly checkpoint to evaluate decision quality.

## Strategic pivot under uncertainty
Default moves:
- Define pivot hypothesis and disconfirming evidence.
- Timebox a validation sprint before full reorg.
- Protect core revenue while testing new direction.

Anti-patterns:
- Narrative pivot without measurable tests.
- Full commitment before signal quality is acceptable.

First 24h actions:
- Write pivot memo with assumptions, metrics, and kill criteria.
- Launch one customer test and one distribution test.
- Set a 14-day review checkpoint with go/no-go decision rules.
