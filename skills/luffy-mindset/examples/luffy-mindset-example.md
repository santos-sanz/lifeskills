# Example: launch a solo product studio in 6 months

## Dream Goal
- Build a solo product studio that reaches the first 10 paying clients within 6 months.

## Current Wall
- Main obstacle: fear of leaving a stable routine and inconsistent sales confidence.
- Real constraint: only 12 hours per week are available outside current obligations.

## Luffy Reframe
- You do not need perfect confidence to start. You need proof through action. The constraint is not a prison; it is a training weight. We win by executing small, high-leverage moves every week.

## Top 3-7 Actions
1. Define one clear niche offer and one target customer profile by Friday.
2. Publish a simple one-page offer with scope, price range, and contact method.
3. Reach out to 15 qualified prospects in 10 days using a short, direct message.
4. Run 5 discovery calls and document repeated pain points.
5. Convert findings into one refined offer and one testimonial request loop.

## Risks and Countermoves
- Risk: low response rate from outreach.
  Countermove: test two message variants and improve list quality after every 10 sends.
- Risk: overbuilding before validation.
  Countermove: do not add features until at least 3 prospects confirm the same pain.

## First 24 Hours
- Immediate action: draft the niche statement and outreach message version 1.
- Time block: 90 minutes tomorrow at 07:30.

## Commitment Line
- "I will send the first 5 outreach messages by tomorrow night, no excuses."

## Reality check note
- No market-size numbers are assumed here because none were provided.
- If the user shares conversion history or pricing data, the next plan can include quantified targets.
