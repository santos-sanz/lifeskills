# Negotiation checklist with pre-mortem control

## Before the conversation
- Objective, minimum acceptable, and walk-away defined.
- BATNA documented and realistic.
- ZOPA range estimated with conservative and optimistic bounds.
- Decision authority map confirmed.
- Conditional concession ladder prepared.
- Pre-mortem completed: likely failure modes and responses.

## During the conversation
- Start with tactical empathy label.
- Use calibrated questions to uncover constraints.
- Mirror key phrases and pause.
- Confirm approval process and criteria.
- Capture commitments and open points in real time.

## Close and follow-up
- Summarize terms, owners, and dates.
- Confirm next checkpoint in writing.
- Record unresolved risks and trigger points for walk-away.

## Pre-mortem prompts
- What could make this deal collapse at the last minute?
- Which assumption, if wrong, invalidates our current strategy?
- What concession could we regret after signing?
