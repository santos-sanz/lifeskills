# SCQA narrative

Use SCQA to create a compelling storyline before the answer.

- Situation: the baseline context everyone agrees on.
- Complication: the change or problem that disrupts the situation.
- Question: the decision or uncertainty created by the complication.
- Answer: the key message or recommendation.

Example:
- Situation: We lead the EU widget market.
- Complication: A low-cost entrant is eroding share.
- Question: How do we protect share without margin collapse?
- Answer: Launch a value tier and automate production.
